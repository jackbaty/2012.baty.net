Stuff I Use

Mar 3rd, 2012

I use a lot of stuff - applications, gadgets, services, etc. Not everything is listed here of course, just the things I find most useful.

(Last updated: 12/24/2011 - added X100)

Software

  • Tinderbox - Writing, planning, tracking and thinking
  • DEVONthink - Email Archives, Manuals, PDFs, Scanned documents
  • Evernote - Digital Detritus
  • OmniFocus - Task Management/GTD
  • PersonalBrain - “No limits Mindmapping”
  • BBEdit - General text editing
  • Safari - Only browser I need
  • Sparrow - ‘cause Apple’s Mail stopped working well for me.
  • Twitterrific - Twitter client
  • Dropbox - File syncing
  • Lightroom - Image editing and management
  • Capture One Pro - Raw conversions
  • Photo Mechanic - Ingesting, tagging, renaming photos from card
  • Silverfast Ai - Scanning software for Kodachrome and the HowTek
  • Vuescan - Scanning software for Epson
  • JIRA - Issue tracking and iteration planning
  • OmniPlan - Project planning and forecasting
  • 1Password - Password management
  • Acorn - Image editing for normal people
  • Chronosync - drive and file backups
  • CSSEdit - in case I absolutely have to edit CSS
  • Transmit - (S)FTP client
  • Skitch - Screenshots
  • Transmission - Bittorrent client
  • Textexpander - Key shortcuts
  • Alarms - Awesome little reminder app
  • Alfred - App launcher and thing finder
  • Keyboard Maestro - Macro wizardry
  • Day One - Personal Journal
  • Remember? - Best calendar app since MacOS 7-ish.

  • Nikon F6 - World’s best 35mm SLR
  • Leica M6 - Rangefinder camera
  • Leica M4 - Rangefinder camera
  • Hasselblad 500 C/M - Medium format film camera
  • Nikon D700 - Digital SLR
  • Fujifilm X100 - Go-everywhere digital
  • Olympus E-P1 - Micro 4/3rds Digital
  • Olympus E-P3 - Micro 4/3rds Digital
  • Minolta Autocord - Medium format TLR
  • Olympus OM-1n
  • Olympus OM-2n
  • Ricoh GR1 - Terrific point and shoot film camera
  • Canon 1Ds - Digital SLR camera
  • Canon Canonet - Rangefinder camera
  • (2) Olympus Stylus Epics - Compact film cameras
  • Epson V750 Pro - Flatbed scanner
  • HP Photosmart 7960 - Photo printer
  • Howtek ScanMaster 4500 drum scanner

Computers and Electronics

  • Apple 27” iMac - A monster.
  • Apple 11” Macbook Air - best computer ever made (so far)
  • Apple iPad - Mostly for running Omnifocus and watching movies
  • Amazon Kindle - Books, lots of ‘em
  • iPhone 4 - The Jesus Phone
  • Rega P1 Turntable
  • Rega Brio Integrated Amplifier
  • Thorens TD 160 Turntable
  • Marantz Receiver (from sometime in the mid-1970s)
  • Sonos wireless music system - Music everywhere in my house
  • Fujitsu ScanSnap - Document scanning
