Rageboy Limericks

May 8th, 2003

Rageboy gives us [5

more ways to leave your lover](//www.rageboy.com/2003_03_01_blogger-archive.html#200064527), and some limericks. My favorite…

There once was an artist perverse

who only could speak in reverse.

Whenever she said,

“No, our love is not dead,”

she really meant “Call in the hearse.”
